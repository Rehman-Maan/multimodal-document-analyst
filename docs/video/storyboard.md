# Demo Storyboard

## Scene 1: Workspace

Show the dashboard metrics and workflow steps.

Message: "A document operations workspace, not a one-off chatbot."

## Scene 2: Upload

Upload an invoice or receipt from `sample_uploads/`.

Message: "Files are validated and processed asynchronously."

## Scene 3: Extraction

Open the document detail page and run structured extraction.

Message: "Schema-based fields include confidence, source page, and validation status."

## Scene 4: Review

Open the review queue and correct or approve an uncertain field.

Message: "Humans stay in the loop when values are uncertain."

## Scene 5: Chat

Ask a question and show the focused citation.

Message: "Answers are grounded in document evidence."

## Scene 6: Export and Evaluation

Export JSON/CSV and open the evaluation report.

Message: "The output is measurable and ready for downstream workflows."
